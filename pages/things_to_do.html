Things to Do in Līči

Explore Nature and Parks

Discover the serene beauty of Līči's surrounding landscapes. Visit the nearby forests for hiking, berry picking, and mushroom gathering. Explore the picturesque countryside that highlights Latvia's natural charm.

Historic Sites

Take a short trip to the historic Rundāle Palace, a Baroque masterpiece with lush rose gardens. Nearby, you can also explore medieval castles such as Sigulda Castle, perfect for history enthusiasts.

Local Cuisine

Indulge in traditional Latvian dishes like smoked fish, rye bread, and seasonal berry desserts. In Līči, you'll find cozy cafes and guesthouses offering authentic meals prepared with local ingredients.

Popular Attractions

For a relaxing experience, visit the Livonian Coast, known for its pristine beaches and tranquil fishing villages. Alternatively, head to Sigulda for thrilling activities like bungee jumping and bobsleighing.

Events and Culture

Join in on local events, such as Midsummer festivities, featuring traditional songs, dances, and bonfire jumping. Experience the warmth of Latvian traditions right in the heart of Līči.
