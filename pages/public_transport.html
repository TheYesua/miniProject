Public Transport in Līči

Bus Services

Līči is well-connected to nearby towns and cities via bus services. Below are the main routes:

  • Route 1: Līči to Riga Central Station
  • Route 2: Līči to Jūrmala
  • Route 3: Līči to Rīga Airport

For more details about bus schedules and fares, visit Riga Satiksme.

Train Services

The nearest train station is located in the town of Jūrmala, approximately 15 minutes from Līči by bus. The train network connects Līči to Riga and other destinations in Latvia.

  • Route 1: Līči to Riga Central Station
  • Route 2: Līči to Jelgava

Taxi Services

For those who prefer a more direct route, taxi services are available throughout Līči. You can call a local taxi company or use apps like Bolt and RedCab Taxi for rides in and around Līči.
